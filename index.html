About us

At our core, we strive to connect users directly with nurses, eliminating the need for intermediaries. Our platform empowers you to find the right nurse at your convenience, on your terms, and with the specific details you need. We established this platform after recognizing the challenges users face in accessing nursing care and the exploitation by companies that take large commissions.
